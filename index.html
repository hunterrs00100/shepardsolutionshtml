Success Story

Verdi NZ - Agricultural Technology Platform

Agriculture • Technology • Sustainability

The Challenge

Verdi needed to establish comprehensive operational systems to deliver soil carbon measurement and verification services to New Zealand farmers, while building a sustainable business model around carbon certificates and farm sustainability consulting.

The Solution

I supported Verdi in designing their operating model, establishing service delivery processes, and creating systems to measure, verify, and enhance farm soil carbon levels while providing farmers with actionable data for sustainable practices.

The Results

Verdi now successfully helps farmers transition to sustainable practices, monetize their carbon improvements, and reduce Scope 3 emissions for corporate clients - creating value across the entire agricultural supply chain.
